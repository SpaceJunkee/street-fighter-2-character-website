Vega was born to a privileged noble family. For reasons unknown, his family status dwindled, causing Vega's beautiful mother to remarry for financial security.

He didn't like his step-father and thought he was ugly. As Vega matured, he studied bull fighting and later went to Japan to study his other passion, Ninjutsu, a style he believed would mesh well with his natural grace and agility.

Combining bullfighting and Ninjutsu, Vega felt his fighting abilities were complete and joined an underground cage fighting circuit. He quickly became one of the best.
